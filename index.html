Welcome to Startup Icon

Starting today, the first day of summer 21 June 2016, we are starting a global, open source initative, to create the icon graphic for startups. The icon used the most is the rocket, however we do not think this is globally recognized, so in the following months we are going to push designers, creative people and startup fans to send us their design for the STARTUP ICON

Submit your design

You can submit your design by using the following form. We will collect designs until the 21st of september, and then we will start the voting.

This project is proudly hosted on github. documentation or contact support and we’ll help you sort it out.
